⭐ Privacy Policy ⭐

Last updated: 6th of January, 2026

Welcome to Trend Clone!

Memory Lab Inc. ("Trend Clone," "we," "us," and/or "our") operates the Trend Clone mobile and web applications, including https://trendclone.app (the "Website"), and other related services (collectively, the "Services").

We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard information through our Services, including your interactions with your AI generated content.

We recognize the sensitive nature of the videos and personal data you entrust to us. At Trend Clone, we aim to provide a secure platform where you can create AI-generated videos based on your personal photos or videos.

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@trendclone.app.

1. Information We Collect

To create a personalized AI videos and provide our Services, we collect certain types of information:

1.1 Information You Provide

When using our Services, you may provide the following:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password. If you log in using third-party services (e.g., Google or Apple), we collect your login credentials and, depending on your settings, details such as your name and email address.
  • Content: When creating a video, you may share details like your photo or video.
  • Payment Information: If you subscribe to premium services, we collect transaction details through our payment processor, but we do not store credit card details.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

We collect certain data automatically when you use our Services:

  • Device Information: Includes details about your device (e.g., model, operating system, browser type, IP address, and language settings).
  • Usage Data: Includes information about how you interact with the Services, such as page visits, clicks, and session duration.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage trends. For more details, please review our Cookie Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

2.1 Provide and Personalize Services

  • Create and maintain your account and personalized videos.
  • Facilitate meaningful interactions with your videos and provide a personalized user experience.
  • Store photos, and generated videos for your long-term access.

2.2 Enhance Security

  • Monitor and protect the integrity and security of our Services, including preventing unauthorized access and fraud.

2.3 Improve the Platform

  • Analyze trends and usage data to improve our Services.
  • Develop new features and optimize performance.

2.4 Communicate with You

  • Send you updates, service-related notifications, and promotional messages.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.

2.5 Ensure Compliance

  • Enforce our Terms of Service and comply with applicable laws and regulations.

We can use or share the content of your interactions for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information under the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party providers to deliver our Services, including:

  • Cloud storage providers for data hosting and management.
  • Payment processors to handle subscriptions and transactions.

3.2 Legal Obligations

We may disclose your information to comply with legal requirements, enforce our policies, or protect the rights and safety of our users.

3.3 Business Transactions

In the event of a corporate transaction, such as a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred to the involved parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the Services or fulfill legal obligations. You can request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting support@trendclone.app.

5. Your Rights

5.1 Access and Updates

You can access and update your account information directly through the platform.

5.2 Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your personal data, including your photos, videos and its associated content. Deleting your account will permanently erase all data.

5.3 Opt-Out

You may opt out of marketing communications by following the instructions in our emails or contacting us.

5.4 Limit Sensitive Data

You may choose not to share sensitive information during interactions.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. Please review this page periodically for the latest information.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:
Email: support@trendclone.app
Website: https://trendclone.app
