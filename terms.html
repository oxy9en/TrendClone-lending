1. OUR MISSION & SERVICES

At Trend Clone, our mission is to provide a platform where anyone can create their own AI-powered viral videos from any links on other social videos or just by choosing any videos from our motion library. Through Trend Clone, users can choose a video from the library or via their own url, add their photos and generate the same videos, but already with their own visuals inside. Users can download the videos or share them via personal links.

1.1 Key Features

  • Motion Library
    A place where we collect all trending videos, so users can choose them to regenerate videos based on their materials and prompts.
  • Adding videos
    Via a url link to any social videos (from Instagram, Tiktok, X, Youtube and others), by filming their own video or choosing from a gallery on the user's phone.
  • Adding photos
    Users can upload their own photos to recreate the original video.
  • Motion prompts
    Users can use the default prompts of the videos or write their own prompts.
  • Pro model
    Users can adjust the quality of generated videos by using standard or premium models.
  • Picture-in-picture
    Users can overlay their generated videos with original video fragmented in the corner.
